My name is Jalves Nicacio and this is my personal homepage.

By passion, I am a programmer and musician. By profession, I am a web developer full stack and professor of computer science. But currently I am usually a PhD student at the University of Québec in Chicoutimi.

I graduated in Computer Science from the Federal University of Alagoas, Brazil, in 2006 and completed my degree in Education (Professional Training) from the Universidade do Sul de Santa Catarina - UNISUL, Brazil (2010). Master in Computer Modeling of Knowledge at the Federal University of Alagoas, Brazil, obtained in 2018.
